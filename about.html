Hello! I'm Rashi Jajodia, a first-year student with a burning enthusiasm for coding and web development. Welcome to my little corner of the internet. Ever since I embarked on my journey as a student, I found myself captivated by the world of programming. The intricacies of coding languages and the art of creating seamless, user-friendly websites have become my source of inspiration and fascination. I am thrilled to connect with fellow enthusiasts, mentors, and anyone who shares a love for technology. Whether you're here to explore my projects, share ideas, or just have a friendly conversation, feel free to connect with me!

I honed my skills through extracurricular activities such as participating in web design competitions, contributing to open-source projects, and designing websites for local businesses. This diverse educational background, along with hands-on experience, has equipped me with a strong foundation in web design and a passion for creating user-friendly and visually appealing digital experiences.

Aside from my passion for coding and web development, I find joy and relaxation in various hobbies that add color to my life. One of my favorite ways to express myself is through the art of dance. The rhythmic movements, the harmony of music, and the sheer joy of dancing allow me to unwind and tap into my creative side. Whether it's the graceful flow of contemporary dance or the energetic beats of hip-hop, I love exploring different dance styles and letting the music guide my movements. Dancing is not just a hobby for me; it's a form of self-expression and a source of endless inspiration.
